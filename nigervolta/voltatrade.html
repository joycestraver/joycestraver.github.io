TRADE VOLTA DELTA

Due to the rivers and the sea in the delta area, lots of trading is being done by the people living in Ghana

Trading activities diver but most of them are natural resource driven. For example, agriculture, fishing, salt mining and arts and crafts.

Agriculture is a big factor in trading in the volta region. The major crops are maize, rice, casava, okra, water melon, tomato, shallots and other vegetables. They also keep a lot of goats, sheep, pigs, chicken and ducks.
